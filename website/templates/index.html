Why BIG?

BIG is the first and largest undergraduate investment club at UC Berkeley. As a student organization we have two goals: to educate our members and have them feel comfortable investing on their own when they graduate, and to manage our fund.

The Fund

We are a student run mutual fund with over $130,000 in assets under management. Our portfolio has regularly beaten out the S&P, averaging a 15% return over the past fiscal year.

Our Network

BIG alumni have graduated to work in a variety of hedge funds, private equity firms, VCs, asset managers, and investment banks. Our network includes professionals from BlackRock, Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley, and many more.
